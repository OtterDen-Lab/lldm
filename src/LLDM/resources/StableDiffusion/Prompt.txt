Ray, the Dragonborn Sorcerer, delves into a dark cave. His meager hit points don't deter him. Casting light to brighten the way, he uncovers ancient inscriptions, detailing the history of a mighty dragon. Goblins attack, but Ray's agility saves him. His fiery magic obliterates the foes. In a hidden chamber, a magical barrier guards a treasure chest. Ray dispels it, revealing the long-lost artifact his clan craves. Triumphantly, he heads back to his kin.
